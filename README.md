# DTS224-Group17

## **Vendor Installation &amp; Restocking Tracking System(VIRTS)**
This is a group project for DTS224 for Group 17 of the Undergraduate Students of Computer Science for set 2024-2028, the aim of this project is to create a Database Application(Application that invokes a database at least once during It's Operation). 
We are a group of 15 members, only the developer team made it to the repo.

### Project Planning
The project was met with different stages namely:
- **Planning**: Ideating around the business who may need the system, **God's wish Boutique** was our choice.
- **Requirements Elicitation**: Me and the elicitation team, went to the business physical location and obtained an unstructured rule.
- **Analysis**: We strctured the business rule and enquired more for gaps that were noticed.  Did a short feasibiliy check and it was great.
- **Design**: Design has begun already, creating the:
  - Entity Relational Diagram.
  - Relational Data Model.
  - Functional Dependency.
  - Enhanced Entity Relational Diagram.

- **Implementation:** Begin by normalizing the data into:
  - First Normal Form.
  - Second Normal Form.
  - Third Normal Form.
  - Boyce Codd Normal Form
Development will begin shortly..
