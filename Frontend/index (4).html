₦-level accuracy

How it works

From walk-in to
full payment

01

Student places an order

The seller logs the order and the item is immediately pulled from the sales floor into an allocated hold. The student's available balance updates in real time.

02

Payments come in over time

Each payment — cash, card, or Moniepoint transfer — is logged with an immutable reference string. The student can see their live balance on the web panel at any time.

03

Automated alerts fire

At 80% completion or near the semester deadline, the system queues a Telegram message to the student. No one has to remember to chase anyone.

04

100% reached — item released

Once fully settled, the system logs the event, releases the item from hold, and the student collects with their student ID. Not a naira before that point.
